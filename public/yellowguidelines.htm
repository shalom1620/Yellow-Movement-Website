Yellow Movement Principles and Working Guidelines

Yellow Movement is committed to fostering an inclusive, collaborative, and equitable space for all individuals who engage with our initiatives. Our guiding principles shape how we work, make decisions, and uphold our values in all circumstances. By engaging with Yellow Movement, whether through collaboration, volunteering, or support, you acknowledge and respect the following principles:

1. Non-Hierarchical Structure – Yellow Movement rejects traditional vertical hierarchies. We operate on a horizontal, collective model where power and decision-making are shared equally among those involved. No single individual or group holds authority over others, and all contributions are valued equally.

2. Equal Value for All Ideas – We believe that diverse perspectives and ideas are essential to meaningful progress. Everyone, regardless of background, expertise, or level of involvement, has the right to voice their thoughts, propose initiatives, and contribute to discussions. While not every suggestion may be implemented, all contributions will be considered with equal respect.

3. Core Feminist Values Are Non-Negotiable – Yellow Movement is firmly rooted in feminist principles, including gender equality, bodily autonomy, freedom from discrimination, and social justice. While we encourage open dialogue and collaboration, our core values are not subject to compromise, debate, or dilution. Any attempt to undermine these principles will not be entertained.

4. Collaborative and Respectful Engagement – We expect all individuals working with Yellow Movement to engage respectfully and in good faith. This includes maintaining a space free from harassment, discrimination, or harmful behavior. Any form of oppression, including sexism, racism, homophobia, transphobia, ableism, or classism, is strictly against our principles.

5. Transparency and Accountability – We strive for transparency in our decision-making processes and hold ourselves accountable to our community. Feedback, constructive criticism, and suggestions for improvement are always welcome, provided they align with our values and commitment to social justice.

6. No Exploitation or Tokenization – Yellow Movement does not engage in performative activism or symbolic representation. We do not accept collaborations that seek to exploit our platform or use feminist causes for personal or corporate gain. All partnerships must align with our mission and contribute meaningfully to our shared goals.

7. Voluntary Participation and Mutual Support – Those who work with Yellow Movement do so on a voluntary and collaborative basis. While we value contributions and expect a commitment to shared responsibilities, we also recognize the importance of care, well-being, and mutual support. Burnout and overwork are not part of our culture.

By engaging with Yellow Movement in any capacity, you affirm your commitment to these principles. Failure to uphold these values or engage in good faith may result in the discontinuation of collaboration or involvement. For further clarification or discussion regarding these guidelines, please reach out to us directly.

About us

Yellow Movement is a university-based organization committed to advocating for social change, community empowerment, and youth engagement. Rooted in a culture of activism, we strive to promote inclusivity, awareness, and meaningful action within and beyond our campus.

Through collaborative initiatives and student-driven efforts, we aim to inspire individuals to take part in shaping a better, more just society. Join us in making a difference, one step at a time.

Our mission

Yellow Movement is committed to fostering social change, empowering youth, and advocating for justice through activism, education, and community engagement.

As a university-based organization, we strive to create an inclusive space where students can raise awareness, challenge societal norms, and take meaningful action toward a more equitable future.

Our vision

We envision a society where young people are active drivers of positive change, using their voices and skills to promote equality, justice, and empowerment.

Through continuous learning, collaboration, and creative advocacy, Yellow Movement aims to inspire generations to build a more conscious, inclusive, and socially responsible world.

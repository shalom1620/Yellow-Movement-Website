About us

Yellow Movement is a university-based organization committed to advocating for social change, community empowerment, and youth engagement. Rooted in a culture of activism, we strive to promote inclusivity, awareness, and meaningful action within and beyond our campus.

Through collaborative initiatives and student-driven efforts, we aim to inspire individuals to take part in shaping a better, more just society. Join us in making a difference, one step at a time.
